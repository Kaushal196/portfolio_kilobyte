THE

CHALLENGE

Our actual task was to create a platform which deals with management solution, covering from appointments, notes to bookings, packages to your own website and profile. We had to connects everything travellers need – content, technology, service and reporting – in one place.

OUR

APPROACH

To complete this task first we had to put travellers at the heart of business travel, continuously supporting them with solutions that are more engaging and effective. We tried to put all the things at one place for travellers. We defined a structure for whole appilcation and worked on each module to fulfil all the needs. We use Laravel technology to develope this application.

We aimed to make business travel better by making it more connected and complete. At one place ,you can get end-to -end bookings,notifications, true customer service that shape your company's travel future.
