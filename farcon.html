FARCON

THE

CHALLENGE

Our task was to provide a user friendly platform for customers so that they can purchase fresh vegetables from their respective home using farcon app and to provide a digital link between customers and market place where you get vegetables at reduced rates.

OUR

APPROACH

To complete this task first we developed a strategy to deal with farmers directly so that we can provide vegetables at low prices. After that, we developed an android app where customers can find vegetables. We use Lumen Technology for writing API. We provide an Admin Panel for admin to organise all stuffs. To develop admin panel we use Laravel Technology.

We aimed at developing a Farcon app to create a personal market place for customers where they get vegetables at reduced rates. With Farcon, customers just need to choose the vegetable they want and we deliver it in expeditious manner.
